About


Autism spectrum disorder (ASD) is an intricate neurodevelopmental disorder with no specific phenotypes defined by impairments in social communication
and by restricted, stereotyped repetitive behavior and patterns of interests or activities. The individual with ASD may communicate, behave, interact,
and learn differently from most other people. Their ability to learn, think, and solve problem can range from gifted to severely challenged.
